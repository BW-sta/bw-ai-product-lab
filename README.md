## AI Application Product Matrix

### 1. PaperReady 稿准

**PaperReady 稿准** is an SSCI English manuscript pre-submission quality control system.

It serves international academic writing and research-service scenarios, helping authors identify risks before submission, including English expression quality, structure logic, journal-style alignment, and AI-writing-risk concerns.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/paperready/

---

### 2. 同行来了

**同行来了** is an AI short-video generation tool for small local-store owners.

It helps local business owners generate daily video ideas, scripts, voiceover text, storyboard guidance, and shooting tasks based on store type, daily business context, competitor activity, and available materials.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/tonghanglaile/

---

### 3. Veilora 初纱

**Veilora 初纱** is an AI wedding imagery generation product.

It serves wedding-photo style preview, wedding invitation visuals, official announcement images, and emotional commemorative imagery scenarios.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/veilora/

---

### 4. 少年召唤师

**少年召唤师** is a children's skill-card and smartwatch tap-to-battle game.

It combines physical skill cards, smart devices, and AI judgment for children's offline social play, parent-child interaction, and low-screen entertainment scenarios.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/shaonian-summoner/

---

### 5. 云华家书

**云华家书** is a time-delivered family letter and emotional asset preservation service.

It helps families preserve important words, blessings, life advice, and emotional messages for future birthdays, weddings, coming-of-age ceremonies, anniversaries, or legacy moments.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/yunhua-family-letter/

---

### 6. 吉祥星

**吉祥星** is an AI digital-person business decision brain for SMEs.

It focuses on customer risk, payment collection reminders, order priority, business gaps, enterprise knowledge accumulation, and business health decision support.

Official page: https://aiasknow.com/jixiangxing/
